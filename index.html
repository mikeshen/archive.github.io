Latest Projects

InstructAR

A Windows Phone mobile app designed to build interactive, 3D instructables

App

Built with OpenCV and the AruCo marker tracking library. This app provides step-by-step instructions for building 3D model based instructables. Users can move the camera around to gain a different perspective of what they are building. The 3D capabilities and intuitive design make it suitable for complex, detailed instructions that are difficult to express in a 2D plane.

Currently we have working demos with a circuitry breadboard, lego set, furniture guide, and are in the process of building many other 3D instructables. Right now we are on the Windows Store, but we are looking to expand and provide cross-platform support.

This project came in 2nd place in the Fall 2014 UofM Microsoft App Development Challenge!
